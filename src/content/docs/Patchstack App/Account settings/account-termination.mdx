<Aside type="caution">
  The data deleted after the schedule of the termination cannot be recovered!
</Aside>
<br />

If you would like to terminate your account, you can do so by heading to the [account settings page](https://app.patchstack.com/settings/account) and then by clicking the red "Terminate" button under the "Terminate account" section on the right side of the screen.

![](@images/account-termination.png)

When you schedule the termination of your account, the following happens immediately:
- All your sites will be deleted
- Your subscription, if any, will be instantly terminated
- Termination date is set for 30 days in the future

The following happens in 30 days:
- Your user is deleted

## How to cancel the termination?
If you would like to cancel the scheduled termination of your account, simply head to [app.patchstack.com](https://app.patchstack.com) and click on the button that shows up to cancel the termination. Any deleted data is not recoverable.
